Accountant's Guide

  • Single vs Annual Limits: Some sections like 194C and 194I trigger TDS if a single payment crosses a limit, OR if the annual aggregate crosses a larger limit. The system handles this using your previous payments input.
  • No PAN? If the payee doesn't provide a PAN, the system automatically applies a higher TDS rate (usually 20%).
  • Purchase of Goods (194Q): If selected, TDS is only calculated on the amount that exceeds ₹50 Lakhs.
